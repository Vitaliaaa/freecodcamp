About In Jazz

In-Jazz, the official representative of YAMAHA Corporation in Ukraine, was founded in 1993 and is one of the largest importers and distributors of high quality musical instruments, professional sound, lighting and studio computer equipment. We offer a wide range of acoustic musical instruments (grand pianos, pianos, guitars, violins, wind, percussion and percussion instruments), modern electronic instruments (keyboard synthesizers, digital and stage pianos, electronic pianos, tone generators).

Sound amplification equipment (acoustic systems , power amplifiers, digital and analog mixing consoles, sound processors, microphones and headphones), computer peripherals for musicians, musical accessories. Our company is the official representative of such world famous brands as YAMAHA, Amphenol, Boesendorfer, Ernie Ball, JTS, Klotz, Kustom, Music Man, Petrof, Regal Tip, Stagg, Steinberg, Weltmeister, On-Stage Stands, Canto, MarkBass, Genelec, Kuppmen, Los Cabos.

In company stores, customers can purchase the necessary goods, as well as get advice from competent specialists who will help you choose the goods that best meet the needs of the client. In the showrooms of the stores, you can get acquainted in detail with the presented musical instruments, concert sound, lighting and DJ equipment, evaluate their capabilities, sound and functionality. The constant expansion of the range of products in the company's stores ensures the achievement of the main goal - the satisfaction of the interests of all customers.
